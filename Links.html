Company History

Here is a list of three related companies

  • Mengtor:   Every business has a unique success story, and Mengtor Inc. is proud to share its own. Founded in 2009 by just two individuals, Mengtor started out as a wholesale distributor of cellphone parts and has since risen to become one of the most preeminent cell repair parts distributors in the industry. With the wireless industry constantly evolving, Mengtor has kept pace with the latest trends, expanding its selection to include the largest inventory of cellular parts from all major brands in the market.
    Technicians can rely on Mengtor as their go-to source for all their repair needs, from the smallest crew to the most complex component. With a steadfast commitment to delivering exceptional customer service, quality assurance, and competitive pricing, Mengtor Inc. remains a trusted partner to businesses and technicians worldwide.
  • SMWireless   Founded in the heart of Santa Maria California, SMWireless started as a small, family-owned business specializing in cell phone accessories. The founders, two brothers named Miguel and David, saw an opportunity to provide local residents with a convenient and affordable source for phone repairs, accessories, and many other tech services.
  • CPR Wireless    First and foremost, we are problem solvers. When you come to CPR with an electronic device issue, we have the technical aptitude and experience to repair it. Beyond smartphones, we specialize in a wide variety of electronic device repairs, including computers, laptops, game consoles, cameras, mp3 players, and much more. We strive to create solutions where there were not any before and will continue to lead the wireless repair industry as the experts in fast, efficient repairs.
